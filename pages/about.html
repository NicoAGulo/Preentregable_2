History

Kronos: Where Time Fuses with Fashion In the heart of Chacarita, a neighborhood in constant transformation, Kronos was born in 1992. The vision of its founder, Ricardo Rub√©n, was clear: to create a space where fashion transcended trends and became a form of personal expression and challenge. Ricardo, a young designer with a passion for the history of fashion and a forward-looking gaze, began experimenting with classic silhouettes and innovative fabrics. His first collections were an eclectic mix of reinterpreted vintage garments and avant-garde designs that challenged conventions. Over time, Kronos became a reference for those seeking a unique and authentic style. The store, located in an old colonial-style house, was a labyrinth of garments and accessories that invited exploration. Every corner of Kronos told a story, from the mannequins dressed in period costumes to the walls adorned with photographs of past campaigns.
